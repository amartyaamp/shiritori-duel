How Not to Fail

1. The Chain: Word starts with the last letter of the previous word.

2. Size Matters: 3+ letters only. No "ox", "id", or "yo".

3. Race to Zero: Start at 100. Every second left on your timer is subtracted from your score. Submit at -5s? Your score goes UP. Don't be slow.

4. Don't Get Personal: No Proper Nouns. No cities, no brands, no names.

5. The Snob Clause: No stop-words ('the', 'and', 'but').
